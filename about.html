My History

I was born in Tucson, AZ and grew up in the foothills of the Catalina mountains. In elementary school I was introduced to Lego Robotics where I designed a robotic bug and controller so I could guide my robot around the room. Ever since this experience I have been fascinated by robots.

After a short stint in Kansas, I returned to Tucson to attend the University of Arizona and pursue a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering. While in school I decided to simultaneously pursue a Minor in Mathematics as well as an Associates in Machine Tool Technology.

During my senior year of college I competed against my peers in a capstone project in which we were split into interdisciplinary teams in order to work on a project for an actual company. My team was able to take home the top prize of Best Overall Design for our work with MediaMation in the production of a Robotic Fish.

I was the lead mechanical engineer for this project and designed both the skin and motion controls systems utilizing SolidWorks, as illustrated in the video below.
